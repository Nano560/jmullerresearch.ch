Acoustic Cloning About Me

This page centers on our paper published in Physical Review Applied 20, 064014 (2023) .

The paper is freely available from the ETH Research Collection.

An easy-to-read article can be found in the APS Physics "Focus" article.

The following videos show the simulated acoustic wave fields for the same 2D waveguide as in the paper. In each video, different Green's functions are used to extrapolate the wave field.

The data and code supporting this work are available in the Zenodo repository (doi:10.5281/zenodo.16880576).
